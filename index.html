Quick Thesis Writing Advice

  • Outline first: Break your thesis into sections and assign approximate pages to each.
  • Write before editing: Produce draft text quickly; polish during revision week.
  • Use timed sprints: Try focused 50/10 or Pomodoro blocks to keep momentum.
  • Daily goal + log: Set a small page target and record what you completed each day.
  • Keep sources handy: Use a reference manager and insert placeholders while drafting.
